How Our Fare Calculation Works

Our Smart Taxi Fare Estimator uses a comprehensive algorithm that takes into account multiple factors to provide the most accurate fare estimate for taxi services across Tamil Nadu.

Base Fare Calculation

The base fare consists of three primary components:

  • Base Rate: A fixed amount charged at the start of every journey
  • Distance Rate: A per-kilometer charge based on the total distance
  • Time Rate: A per-minute charge based on the estimated duration
Basic Formula:

Base Fare = Base Rate + (Distance Rate × Distance) + (Time Rate × Duration)

Dynamic Adjustment Factors

The base fare is then adjusted based on several real-time factors:

Traffic Conditions

Traffic affects both time and fuel consumption:

  • Low: No adjustment (x1.0)
  • Moderate: 20% increase (x1.2)
  • Heavy: 50% increase (x1.5)
  • Extreme: 80% increase (x1.8)
Weather Conditions

Adverse weather increases difficulty and risk:

  • Clear: No adjustment (x1.0)
  • Cloudy: 5% increase (x1.05)
  • Rain: 20% increase (x1.2)
  • Snow/Storm: 40-60% increase (x1.4-1.6)
Time of Day

Rush hour & late night have higher rates:

  • Day (9AM-4PM): No adjustment (x1.0)
  • Evening (7-10PM): 10% increase (x1.1)
  • Rush Hours: 20-30% increase (x1.2-1.3)
  • Night (10PM-5AM): 25% increase (x1.25)
Demand Levels

High demand leads to surge pricing:

  • Low: 10% decrease (x0.9)
  • Normal: No adjustment (x1.0)
  • High: 30% increase (x1.3)
  • Very High: 80% increase (x1.8)

Final Fare Calculation

Complete Formula:

Final Fare = Base Fare × Traffic Factor × Weather Factor × Time Factor × Demand Factor

For electric vehicles, an eco-friendly discount of 10% may be applied

This comprehensive calculation ensures that our fare estimates reflect real-world conditions and provide fair pricing for both passengers and drivers.
